About Me

Engineering at the frontier of life sciences

With over a decade of experience in biomedical R&D, I specialise in translating complex scientific requirements into precision-engineered hardware. At Miltenyi Biotec I lead mechanical development for next-generation cell therapy instruments — from concept ideation and FEA simulation through to design-for-manufacture and regulatory submission.

My work sits at the intersection of mechanical engineering, rapid prototyping, and cross-disciplinary team leadership. I thrive in environments where the problem space is ambiguous and the stakes are high. Fluent in German and English; conversational in Mandarin Chinese.
